When I found out that I was going to have to work with [3D UI's](./vibia), I recognized the importance of preparation and practice. That's when I came up with the idea of this quick and fun little project.

### From the __README.md:__

> ### &#128213; Introduction
> Welcome to **PoKéDeX**, a simple application for Pokémon enthusiasts of all ages. <br />
> With a mix of 8-bit sprites and dynamic 3D effects, **PoKéDeX** offers a delightful experience.
>
> ### &#128640; Features
> - **Dynamic 3D Effects**: Experience Pokémon 8-bit style sprites rendered in a 3D scene powered by [Three.js](https://threejs.org).
> - **Real-time Pokémon Data**: Access comprehensive information about your favorite Pokémon, sourced from the public [PokéAPI](https://pokeapi.co).
>
> ### &#128308; Demo
> You can find a live demo here: [pokedex.veraserg.io](https://pokedex.veraserg.io).
>
> _NOTE:_ &#128187; this project was developed using [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/).
