## Introduction

Welcome to **MyPoKéDeX**, a simple React application for Pokémon enthusiasts of all ages!
**MyPoKéDeX** offers an engaging experience, with a charming cartoon style and a mix of 8-bit sprites with dynamic 3D effects.

## Features
- **Dynamic 3D Effects**: Experience Pokémon 8-bit style sprites rendered in a 3D scene powered by <a href="https://threejs.org" target="_blank">Three.js</a>.
- **Real-time Pokémon Data**: Access comprehensive information about your favorite Pokémon, sourced from the public <a href="https://pokeapi.co" target="_blank">PokéAPI</a>.
